# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 16, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to DeGrow. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our habit tracking application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address**: For account authentication and communication
- **Display name**: To personalize your experience
- **Profile photo** (optional): If you choose to upload one

### 1.2 Habit Data
We collect and store:
- **Habit names and descriptions**: The habits you create
- **Completion records**: When you mark habits as complete
- **Scheduled days**: Your habit schedules
- **Focus session data**: Timer durations and completion times
- **Reminder preferences**: Your notification settings

### 1.3 Technical Information
We automatically collect:
- **Device information**: Device type, operating system version
- **App usage data**: Features used, session duration
- **Error logs**: Crash reports and technical diagnostics
- **Authentication tokens**: To maintain your logged-in session

### 1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not track your location
- We do not access your contacts
- We do not read your messages or emails
- We do not collect browsing history outside the App

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 To Provide the Service
- Create and maintain your account
- Store and sync your habit data across devices
- Send reminder notifications (if enabled)
- Display your progress and statistics

### 2.2 To Improve the Service
- Analyze app usage patterns to improve features
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features based on user needs

### 2.3 To Communicate With You
- Send important service updates
- Respond to your support requests
- Notify you of changes to our policies

### 2.4 We Do NOT
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Share your habit data with anyone without your consent

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Where We Store Your Data

- Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
- Data is encrypted at rest on our servers

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption**: All data transmission is encrypted
